## Executive summary

Three demo supplies, all currently on **floating** products (effective €0,155–0,168/kWh once the adjustment clause is added). Moving each to a better tariff structure — keeping all regulated/municipal/tax charges unchanged — would have saved, on the sampled bills:

| | Actual | Recommended | Saving | % |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| All 3 demo supplies (2 months each) | **€1.079,65** | **€985,79** | **€93,86** | **8,7%** |

Annualised ≈ **~€560/year**. Biggest levers: the common-areas business supply and the low-consumption apartment.

## Recommendation per supply

| Supply | What | Now | Recommended | Saving |

| **D1** | Main residence, **night tariff**, ~1.000 kWh/mo (HIGH priority) | Floating night (~0,155/0,123) | **Fixed dual-zone** (day 0,145 / night 0,105) — certainty; a floating dual-zone would save more but reintroduces risk | €16,19 (3,0%) |
| **D2** | Apartment, ~290 kWh/mo | Floating (~0,157) | **Zero-πάγιο** plan — at low consumption the standing charge dominates | €25,93 (16,2%) |
| **D3** | Building common areas, business 25 kVA | Floating (~0,168) | **Cheaper business floating** (~0,133); a non-incumbent business *green* (~0,118, zero πάγιο) would save more **if it accepts a κοινόχρηστα meter** | €51,74 (13,6%) |

Note the lessons the skill encodes: at **D2** a zero-πάγιο plan beats a lower-rate/higher-πάγιο fixed one; at **D3** the cheapest option is *floating*, not fixed, and the very cheapest is a non-incumbent gated by meter eligibility.

## How the numbers are built

Each bill splits into four blocks; **only the competitive-supply block changes** when you switch. The backtest replaces that block, keeps regulated charges, municipal fees, ΕΡΤ and taxes exactly as billed, and recomputes VAT at 6% of (supply + regulated + ΕΦΚ). See the project [`SKILL.md`](../.claude/skills/greek-electricity-bill-analysis/SKILL.md) and `example_model.xlsx` (sheets: Bills_Raw, Supplies, Bill_Line_Items, Fee_Model, Market_Offers, Scenario_Model, Backtest, Recommendation).

## Caveats (same as any real run)

- Demo rates are illustrative. Floating (Κίτρινο/Πράσινο) prices reset monthly — verify on **energycost.gr** before signing.
- A business/κοινόχρηστα product must be confirmed to accept your meter (kVA, shared meter) before it counts.
- Fixed-contract exit fees live in each product's "Ειδικοί Όροι".
